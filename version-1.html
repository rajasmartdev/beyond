Our test's highly-reliable methodology yields a simple-to-understand risk result, predicting which adult patients with diabetes are at low, moderate, or high risk for complications over the next 5 years.

When clinicians combine information gathered through beyondA1C and clinical examination, they will know which patients are at higher versus lower risk for complication progression.
