INTRODUCING BEYONDA1C™

One Test. Clear Answers. Better Lives.

Advanced, cost-effective diagnostic test
Designed to complement the traditional A1C
Clinically validated methodology

Our test's highly-reliable methodology yields a simple-to-understand risk result, predicting which adult patients with diabetes are at low, moderate, or high risk for complications over the next 5 years.

When clinicians combine information gathered through beyondA1C and clinical examination, they will know which patients are at higher versus lower risk for complication progression.

Simple Risk Stratification

Three clear levels. Three clear pathways to care

This simple risk score enables prediction of progressive complications. Allowing physicians to optimize treatments and clinical resources.

LOW RISK

Standard Monitoring

Continue with routine care Protocols and regular checkups, Maintain current healthy lifestyle practices and medication adherence

MEDIUM RISK

Enhanced Monitoring

Increase frequency of check-ups and diagnostic testing. Adjust treatment protocols as needed. Implement preventive interventions

HIGH RISK

Intensive Care

Immediate intervention required. Frequent monitoring and specialized care. Comprehensive treatment plan with multidisciplinary team
